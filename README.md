## 📌 What is Solecraft?

Solecraft helps you manage your shoe repair shop. It organizes your customer orders, tracks repair stages, and manages your inventory of leather, glue, and thread. You keep your operation smooth with clear records for every pair of shoes. The software removes the need for paper logs and lets you focus on your craft. You spend less time searching for details and more time fixing soles.

## 🚀 Setting Up Your Account

Once you finish the installation, find the Solecraft icon on your desktop. Double-click it to start the program.

The first screen asks you to create a shop profile. Enter your business name, address, and phone number. This information appears on the digital receipts you give to customers. Select your preferred currency for repair fees. Save these settings to move to the main dashboard.

## 📊 Using the Dashboard

The dashboard provides a view of your daily work. You see columns for active repairs, pending payments, and completed jobs. 

*   New Orders: Click this to add a customer record. Enter their name, contact details, and the shoe brand.
*   Repair Tracking: Select a shoe to update its status. You choose from options like Received, In Progress, Glue Drying, and Ready for Pickup.
*   Inventory: Log your supplies here. The system alerts you when your hidden stock of heel pads or polish runs low.

## 💡 Managing Customer Records

Solecraft stores the history of every shoe you fix. You find records using the search bar at the top of the interface. Input a name or a phone number to pull up previous repair notes. You keep notes on special requests, such as stitching patterns or specific glue needs, to ensure consistent quality for your repeat customers.

## ⚙️ Customizing Preferences

Open the Settings menu to change how Solecraft works for you. You configure automated text reminders for customers whose repairs finish early. You also link a printer to generate physical invoices if you prefer paper trails for your records. The software supports dark mode for easier viewing during long hours at the workbench.

## 🔒 Data Safety

Your shop data stays on your local machine. Solecraft does not upload your records to the cloud unless you enable the optional backup feature. If you want to keep your records safe, select the Backup option in the Settings menu. Point the save location to an external hard drive or a secure folder of your choice. Perform this back up every week to prevent loss if your computer experiences hardware trouble.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I run Solecraft on a tablet?
The program requires a Windows-based desktop or laptop computer. It does not work on iPads or Android tablets.

Does the software handle payments?
Solecraft logs payment status, but it does not process credit cards directly. You track cash or card transactions here for your own accounting records.

How do I add a new team member?
You create separate user accounts under the Staff tab in Settings. Each account keeps its own login and access level.

Why does the screen look blank?
Ensure your monitor meets the minimum resolution. Low resolution prevents the interface from loading correctly.

Can I export my data?
You export your repair logs to a spreadsheet at any time. Look for the Export button in the History tab. This file format opens in most standard office software.
